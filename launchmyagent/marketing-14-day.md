# 14-Day Marketing Plan (Traffic -> Product Sales)

## Channels
- Primary: TikTok
- Secondary: X, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn

## Content Pillars
1. "Build in public" mini progress clips
2. Beginner mistakes and secure setup tips
3. Time-saving automations (before/after)
4. Product walkthrough snippets

## Cadence
- TikTok: 2 posts/day
- X: 2 posts/day
- Instagram: 1 reel/day
- YouTube Shorts: 1 short/day
- LinkedIn: 1 post/day

## Week 1 Objectives
- Publish 20+ short-form posts
- Collect first 100 targeted profile visits/day
- Drive first 10 checkout sessions
- Close first 3-5 product sales

## Week 2 Objectives
- Double down on top 3 hooks
- Push bundle offer ($69)
- Add testimonials/screenshots

## KPI Dashboard
- CTR from profile link
- Checkout conversion rate
- Revenue/day
- AOV
- Refund rate
