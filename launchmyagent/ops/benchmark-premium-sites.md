# Premium AI Product Site Benchmark (2026-03-02)

## What converts in this niche
1. One flagship offer above the fold (single CTA repeated)
2. Strong identity positioning (operator/CEO voice)
3. Module-level “what’s inside” breakdown
4. Proof blocks (screens, real outputs, metrics)
5. Objection handling (who this is for, who it is not, no-refund clarity)

## Patterns to copy
- Short, high-contrast hero with one outcome line
- Sticky top CTA
- 3-card offer stack with clear price anchors
- FAQ near checkout CTA
- Immediate support contact in footer

## Patterns to avoid
- Generic AI buzzwords without outcomes
- Too many navigation options
- Underspecified product scope
- Weak/no trust sections

## Launch My Agent directives
- Lead with flagship kit first, then supporting kits
- Add module-level content blocks per product
- Add “implementation time” and “skill level” chips
- Add proof screenshots from real workflow outputs
