# Daily AI Newsletter Plan (Launch My Agent)
Last updated: 2026-02-27

## Newsletter concept
**Name:** Agent Brief Daily
**Positioning:** 5-minute practical AI brief for builders and operators.

## Format (daily)
1. **One big AI update** (what changed)
2. **Why it matters** (operator impact)
3. **One workflow to try today** (practical action)
4. **One tool/prompt of the day**
5. **One monetization angle** (how to turn it into revenue)

## Free vs Paid
### Free tier
- 3 key bullets/day
- 1 quick workflow tip

### Paid tier ($9-$19/month)
- Full daily brief
- Prompt pack of the day
- Weekly automation template drop
- Monthly office-hours style Q&A digest

## Publishing workflow
- Morning: collect 5 candidate stories
- Filter to top 1-2 practical stories
- Generate draft in house style
- Human skim for accuracy
- Publish + social snippets

## Monetization model
- Subscription revenue
- Product upsell (kits)
- Affiliate links (selective, trustworthy)
- Sponsored placements after audience growth

## 14-day launch plan
- Days 1-3: publish free samples publicly
- Days 4-7: open waitlist + lead magnet
- Days 8-10: launch paid tier founding price
- Days 11-14: testimonials + retention loop
